INTERSPEECH 2026 Submitted Under Review

VorTEX: Various overlap ratio for Target Speech EXtraction

Ro-hoon Oh*, Jihwan Seol*, Bugeun Kim  (* Co-first authors)

Proposes VorTEX, a target speech extraction model robust to various overlap ratios. Introduces the PORTE dataset for overlap-aware evaluation and the SuRE metric for comprehensive performance assessment.

Contribution: VorTEX model design and experiments, PORTE dataset construction, SuRE evaluation metric proposal, experimental result analysis.

arXiv
EMNLP 2025 Main Published

VoiceBBQ: Investigating Effect of Content and Acoustics in Social Bias of Spoken Language Model

Junhyuk Choi, Ro-hoon Oh, Jihwan Seol, Bugeun Kim  (2nd author)

Investigates social bias in spoken language models by constructing VoiceBBQ, a speech-based bias benchmark. Evaluates Qwen2-Audio and LLaMA-Omni, revealing that acoustic cues significantly amplify existing textual biases.

Contribution: VoiceBBQ dataset construction, Qwen2-Audio & LLaMA-Omni experiments, result analysis.
